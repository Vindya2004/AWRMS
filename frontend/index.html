Welcome to Hela Suwaya

Uncover the incredible healing properties of the ancient arts of Ayurveda and Hela Wedakama (Sri Lankan Indigenous Medicine) as you embark on a personalised restorative journey at Hela Suwaya Ayurveda.

Nestled in the former kingdom of Anuradhapura, our Ayurveda retreat in Sri Lanka is an oasis amidst the busy city. Under the guidance of renowned Ayurveda physician, Dr. Sarath Guanawardhana, our treatments programmes are designed to heal you from within and help you reconnect and rejuvenate mind, body and soul.

Wherever you are on your lifelong journey, find personalised guidance and proven expertise to heal and inspire your way of life.

ABOUT US

Begin your transformative and restorative journey at Hela Suwaya. We provide a completely personalised and immersive healing experience.

OUR CONCEPT

Hela Suwaya means ‘the rising sun’ in Sinhalese. Our concept of healing is rooted in holistic care for mind, body, and soul.

  • Holistic treatments to improve mental and physical wellbeing.
  • Customised treatments by Ayurvedic physicians.
  • Combining Ayurveda and Hela Wedakama practices.
